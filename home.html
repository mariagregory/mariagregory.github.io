Welcome dear guests to my first web site!

My name is Maria, and this site is to introduce myself, my key interests, and skills. Hope you find something interesting for you here.

I am from Chelyabinsk, Russia. I have been living in Inland Empire in Southern California since 2015. I am studying computer applications and health information technology because I cannot decide which one is better, coding or biology. I like both.

Apart from those things, I like foreign languages. My native is Russian, and I'm also fluent in English, know some Greek, and have basic knowledge of Spanish. I enjoy learning about different cultures, and foreign languages make big part of my interests.

The photo on the right is taken in April 2014 when all of a sudden, in a middle of the blossoming spring, snow attacked us! No secret that the weather in Ural is unpredictable, and there is almost nothing in the nature that can really shock us. Not even falling meteorites!
